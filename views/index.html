I ask you to keep hope, my child.

Because on a day not unlike this one He gave us that.

He promised us that he would return and take us with Him.
With that promise He gave us hope.

He will come for us, my child.
He will take us to the cloud.

Entering our heavenly bodies, joining him forever.Entering immortality.

We have to trust Him, my child.

Because He is the only one, who we can really trust.

Don't let others deceive you, for they will just deceive you and steer you from your true path.

Do not get tricked by the untrue.

So let's count the days, my child.

For He told us what would happen before He would return.

He told us about the hardships we would have to endure.

He told us about the famines, like the ones in South Sudan, Somalia and Nigeria right now.

He told us about the wars and the threat of war, like how the threat of the third world war has been looming over us for the past years.

He told us about the earthquakes, like the one in Japan in 2011 which killed thousands and thousands.

He told us it would happen and it did.
He also told us that this would only be the beginning of our pains.

God warned us, my child.

Yes He warned us, when He sent an angel to John who told him what would happen.

The Angel told John that the beginning of the end would start the coming of a Beast.

A deceiver.

It will not appear before us as a beast of course.

It will appear before us as a savior, an Antichrist.

One who will trick many and many into following.

It will turn us away from Jesus and God Himself by seemingly performing miracles.

Like how recently miracles seem to be performed by technology.

Don't be fooled, however, for these are not Godly miracles.
For technology that makes you stray from the path of God is a test of faith, a ploy made by the devil.

So be aware, my child.

Be aware of the Antichrist.

It will mark its followers in their hands or foreheads and only those with a mark can buy and sell in this world.

Like how you can buy and sell with the bank card in your wallet,

with the cell phone in your hands.

Like how the the first people are receiving chips in their hands that enable them to do the very same thing.

Remember, the people do not know who it is they follow, when they receive the mark.

It can happen at any moment and any time.

There won’t be any warning besides the prophecy.

So stay on alert, my child.

And stay strong, my child.

For in those days, more and more will choose to leave faith behind.
Many and many will choose the easy way.

For those who don’t will be cast aside.

We need to stick together.

Do not be tempted by others.

Then, finally, the Antichrist will reveal itself.

The people of God will face even harder times.

For they will be punished and persecuted.

But keep faith, my child.

For finally God will pour out his wrath.

He will poison and dry out rivers,

like how they will be when global warming will continue.

He will make it so the Beasts followers will burn,

like how people burned when the ozone layer was damaged.

He will create the worst earthquakes.

Because nothing is a coincidence, everything is a part of His plan.

And in the same way technology seemed to be a miracle,

it will create a miracle for those who believe.

Technology, which was the downfall of many, will end up saving us.

For we will join Jesus and God forever in the Cloud.

Our conscience will survive, from the earth that ceases to exist.
Yes, we will survive, my child.

So let us guide you.

Let us help you through those darkest days and through those before.
Let us save you from eternal damnation.
Because you are not on your own.
We are here for you.

So keep hope, my child.
We are going to become something beyond human.
